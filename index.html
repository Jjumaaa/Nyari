About Our Church

Discover who we are and what we believe

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through authentic worship, intentional discipleship, and compassionate service.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, multi-generational community that transforms lives through the power of the Gospel and impacts our city for Christ.

Our Values

Biblical truth, authentic relationships, spiritual growth, generous living, and serving others with compassion.

Our Story

Founded in 1985, PCEA NYARI Church began as a small Bible study in a living room with a vision to reach the growing suburban community. Through God's faithfulness, we've grown into a vibrant congregation serving thousands each week.

Our commitment remains the same - to share the life-changing message of Jesus Christ in relevant ways that connect with people where they are.

Learn More About Our History
Our original building dedication in 1988

Our Core Beliefs

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe in the deity of Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and personal return.

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone, not by works, and results in a transformed life.

New Here?

We're so glad you're considering visiting Grace Community Church

1

Plan Your Visit

Check our service times and location. Services last about 90 minutes. Dress is casual - come as you are!

2

Arrive Early

Arrive 15 minutes early to park and check in children. Our greeters will welcome you and answer any questions.

3

Enjoy the Service

Experience uplifting worship and practical biblical teaching. Don't feel pressured to participate - just observe if you prefer.

4

Visit the Welcome Center

After service, stop by our Welcome Center for a free gift and to learn more about our church.
